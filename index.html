Enter the figures from your latest merchant statement, add the Windcave rates being offered, and press Calculate. We'll show what you paid versus what the same volume would cost on Windcave. Nothing leaves your browser.

Where do I find these numbers? (open me first)

Your statement lists each card type with money columns for net sales, interchange, scheme and margin. Group the lines into the four rows below and copy across the totals. Ignore the minus signs.

Visa Domestic = Visa debit + credit (local) Mastercard Domestic = Mastercard debit + credit (local) Visa / Mastercard International = overseas-issued cards

This calculator covers Mastercard, Visa, DCC and Epal/EFTPOS only. Other payment types — Discover, UnionPay, Amex — are not included in the calculation.

How to read this: Interchange is set by Visa/Mastercard and passed through at cost on both sides. Windcave bills scheme at a fixed 0.14% of Visa volume and 0.19% of Mastercard volume (applied in the background). The acquiring margin you enter applies to all volume; the international loading is added on top for overseas cards. This is an indicative monthly comparison from the figures entered, shown ex-GST; GST applies on top of both providers' fees and doesn't change the saving. Fixed costs (terminal/gateway, monthly minimums, PCI, chargebacks) sit outside this view.
